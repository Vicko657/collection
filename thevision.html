OUR VISION

We asked each of the four founders of "The Four Collection" why they have chosen to take this venture and the meaning of their slogan "Love, Grace, Liberty & Creativity."

WHY?

LEKAN:

LOVE.

For us this is another form of evangelism to the whole of the outside world - not just Christians about Jesus' love. We want to change the perception of religion to relationship with God.

DEVONTE:

GRACE.

We are exposing people to different ways of experiencing Christ's grace - intimacy with Him and His healing touch: His love, His peace and His joy.

OYE:

LIBERTY.

We want people to be able to carry and express Christ with them whenever they go; in their pocket , on their phone, on their clothing and on their hearts.

"always carrying about in the body the dying of the Lord Jesus, that the life of Jesus also may be manifested in our body."

2 Corinthians 4:10

EPHRAIM:

CREATIVITY.

As a whole, our goal is to represent the various different natures of Christ. His creativity through every product we create.

This is our message.

To be living representations and ambassadors of Christ in whatever way possible.

"You are our epistle written in our hearts, known and read by all men"

II Corinthians 3.2
